1.Spinach and Corn Sandwich

Once you discovered the manifold benefits of including spinach in your regular diet, you begin to realise that Popeye was really on to something. It's a powerful antioxidant, packed with nutrients and low on fat. And when you throw corn into the mix, known for enhancing heart health and cutting cholesterol, the outcome is a dish perfect for snacking while being mindful of the calorie count. This Youtube video recipe tutorial by HomeCookingShow is the perfect guide to follow if you want to make your own batch of healthy spinach and corn sandwiches at home!

Ingredients:
Bread
Unsalted Butter
Mint Chutney
Cheese Slice
To Make Filling
Butter - 1 Tbsp
Chopped Garlic - 1 Tsp
Green Chili - 1 No. Chopped
Onion - 1 No. Finely Chopped
Cooked Sweet Corn Kernel - 1/2 Cup
Salt - 1/4 Tsp
Pepper
Chopped Spinach - 1/2 Bunch
Maida - 1 Tsp
Milk - 1/2 Cup
Cheese Slice

Steps:
Heat a pan with some butter, add garlic, onions and saute them
Add cooked corn kernels and saute along
Add salt and pepper and mix well
Add chopped spinach leaves and cook them
Add maida, milk and mix everything well
Finally, add a slice of cheese and mix it
The corn and spinach filling is done, keep it aside
Take 2 slices of bread, spread butter and mint chutney on both the sides.
Place the cheese slice on top of it place the filling and close it with be ready.
Heat a grill pan with some butter, place the sandwich on it
Apply some butter on top of it and grill the sandwich till it turns to a golden brown color on both sides
Serve the sandwich hot.


2. Toasted Hummus Sandwich/b>

I don't know about you, but I can finish an entire jar of hummus in one sitting alone, such is the awesome pairing of tang and texture in this Middle Eastern delicacy. The fact that the chick-pea based dish is packed with nutrients like protein, vitamins and minerals, is another cherry on the delicious cake. So, as far as healthy sandwich spreads go, hummus is a strong contender. Add some toasted bread and crunchy veggies, and your hunger pangs are well taken care of. We loved this Youtube recipe tutorial by Tarla Dalal, which gives you an informational step-by-step breakdown to putting together your own platter.

Ingredients:
To be mixed blended hummus (using 3 Tbsp Water)
1 cup soaked and boiled kabuli chana (white chick peas)
2 tbsp olive oil
2 garlic cloves (lehsun)
¼ cup curds (dahi)
Salt to taste
12 toasted bread slices
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp finely chopped garlic (lehsun)
¼ cup finely chopped onions
½ cup finely chopped coloured capsicum
½ cup finely chopped broccoli
1 tbsp finely chopped basil
1 tsp dried oregano
2 tsp dry red chilli flakes
1 tbsp grated processed cheese
salt to taste
6 tsp butter for spreading

Steps:
1. Heat the olive oil in a deep non-stick pan, add the garlic and onions and sauté on a medium flame for 1 minute.
2. Add the capsicum and sauté on a medium flame for 1 minute.
3. Add the broccoli, basil, oregano, chilli flakes, cheese and salt and sauté on a medium flame for 1 minute.
4. Switch off the flame, cool the mixture slightly, add the prepared hummus and mix well.


3.High-Protein Cheese Sandwich

As any religious gym-goer will be quick to tell you, protein is the single most important diet component you need when you're building your muscles. So, we found an easy to make a sandwich that packs a serious high-protein punch! This sandwich recipe makes use of delicious cottage cheese to get your fill of protein, along with B Vitamins and minerals like calcium, selenium, and phosphorus. Our favourite recipe on the internet would have to be this Youtube video tutorial by Sooraj Mohan, due to its simple, minimalistic procedure accompanied by detailed guidelines and useful commentary!

Ingredients:
Whole Grain or Gluten Free Bread
Butter or whole30 mayo
Avocado, mashed
Roma tomato, sliced
Cucumber, sliced
Full-fat cottage cheese
Salt and Pepper

Steps:
Spread
Lay out your slices of bread on a cutting board or plate. Spread the butter onto the bread. Next, spread the mashed avocado onto the bread, or lay the slices overtop of the butter.
Add the Veggies
Lay the tomato and cucumber slices overtop of the avocado and a pinch of salt and pepper.
Add the Cheese
Finally, spread the cottage cheese over top of the veggies. Finish it off with another pinch of salt and pepper and close it up with the other slice of bread.
Serve
DEVOUR your delicious sandwich!


4. Beetroot Sandwich

On your journey to a healthier version of yourself, beetroot is one of the most nutritious and beneficial vegetables you can reap the perks of, which include plenty of fibre, folate, manganese and potassium. Besides, beetroot when cooked as wonderfully as shown in this Youtube video recipe tutorial by Rajan Singh Jolly, comes with a lip-smacking taste! While this one makes use of mayo, ketchup and oregano, you can choose condiments that appeal to your tastebuds, and curate a mouthwatering yet diet-friendly plate of beetroot sandwiches for lunch.

Ingredients:
6-8 slices bread, edges sliced off
1 small carrot, peeled, grated & water squeezed out
1 beetroot, peeled, grated & water squeezed out
1 small onion, chopped
1 small bell pepper, deseeded & chopped
4 tbsp veg mayonnaise
1 tbsp tomato sauce
1/2 tsp oregano spice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper powder

Steps:
add the mayonnaise & rest of the ingredients in a bowl & mix till they combine evenly
2. on the bread slices spread a medium thick layer of the mixture.
3. make a double slice by inverting one layed slice over the other & repeat with the rest
4. cut the bread slices diagonally & serve
