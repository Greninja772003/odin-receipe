1. No-Bake Cheesecake with Oat Crust

A treat for parents and children, here's a decadent 3-ingredient, and guess what, we donâ€™t use gelatine or heavy cream in the filling. The best part, it is ready in 10 minutes. Isn't it already making you crave?

Ingredients:
Cream cheese (Order gluten-free cream cheese from Epigamia!)
Honey
Vanilla
Oats
Butter
Cinnamon

Steps
In a bowl, add cream cheese, honey and vanilla, and whip until you get a smooth mixture. Keep it aside.
Take a blender or a food processor and add oats, butter, cinnamon and some honey.
Blend till you get a crumbly mixture that sticks together.
It's time to combine both the mixtures. In a cupcake mould, gently create a base with oats mixture.
Now smear the base with the prepared cream cheese filling.
Top it off with strawberry slices and refrigerate for 2-3 hours.
Serve cold!


2. Ingredient Chocolate Fudge

Yes, we are talking about a healthy version of chocolate fudge, and that too, using two ingredients. Well, I am definitely trying this dessert today, what about you?

Ingredients:
Dark Chocolate
Condensed Milk

Steps
Grease your baking pan with melted butter and line it with parchment paper.
Cut your dark chocolate into small bits.
Add condensed milk and the chopped chocolate to a heavy-bottomed pan.
Place over heat and stir till the chocolate melts into a smooth mixture.
Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the baking pan and smoothen.
Refrigerate for 3-4 hours.


3. Strawberry Frozen Yoghurt

No one will ever say no to this health-right dessert in the form of strawberry frozen yoghurt. It's easy to make and tasty to devour.

Ingredients:
750g strawberries, hulled
1/2 cup honey.
500g plain fat-free yoghurt (Order gluten-free, vegan yoghurt from Epigamia!)

Steps
Process strawberries and honey until smooth.
Strain through a fine sieve into a large jug.
Discard the solids and return the mixture to the processor.
Add yoghurt to the mix and process until smooth.
Pour the same into two 3 cup-capacity plastic containers.
Cover the surface with plastic wrap and freeze for 4 hours or until frozen.
Remove from the freezer and let it stand for 3 minutes or until just softened.
Spoon into the processor and process until smooth.
Transfer to a 6 cup-capacity plastic container and cover the surface with plastic wrap.
Again freeze for 4 hours or till firm.
Remove from the freezer and let it stand for 3 minutes or until it softens.
Serve with love!


4. Sugar-free Healthy Kulfi

This year, surprise your folks with a low calorie, sugar-free, vitamin E and calcium-rich healthy.

Ingredients:
1.5 cups + 1 cup Almond milk (Order unsweetened plant-based almond milk from Epigamia!)
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp rice flour
20 almonds
1/2 tsp cardamom powder
A pinch of cinnamon powder
4 strands of saffron
1 tsp rose water
2 tsp pistachio, crushed coarsely

Steps
Boil water, add 20 almonds to it. Boil for 2 minutes. Cool and peel them after 2 minutes. Blend it to a smooth paste and keep it aside.
Heat the milk to a boil with saffron strands and then simmer for 25 - 35 minutes till it thickens.
When it thickens, add the blended almonds and heat for 2-4 minutes.
Add crushed almonds, pistachios, sugar, cardamom powder, cinnamon powder and place the milk over the heat for two minutes.
Dissolve 1 tsp of rice flour in 2 tsps of water. Add that to the mixture.
When it is totally thick, switch off and let it cool completely.
Now blend it once more for a perfect creamy texture.
Keep it in the refrigerator for an hour.
Set in any containers you want, cover and freeze it.
Pour the ice cream into individual kulfi moulds and freeze till set (for 6-8 hrs).
Unmold the kulfi by placing it under running water for a few seconds.
Remove on a plate, slice the kulfi, garnish with pistachios and serve immediately.
